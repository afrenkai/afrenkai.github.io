Hi, I'm Artem!

I am an incoming MS student in NYU Courant's Data Science program. I received my BS in Data Science from Worcester Polytechnic Institute.

My research interests are representation learning, discrete diffusion models, and self-supervised learning. I am also interested in non-convex optimization, mechanistic interpretability, and non-autoregressive language models as a whole! I think that we leave a lot on the table focusing only on token/pixel space, and hope to explore that in my work. I am always on the lookout for research collaborations, so don't hesitate to send me an email.
